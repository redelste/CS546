My Education

Carmel Del Mar Elementary

An elementary school built, and architecturally inspired by the castles found in the Scotish Highlands, and located in Southern California. As this was an elementary school, my favorite class was science class.

Canyon Crest Academy

A modern institution with an open campus layout with multiple buildings, and a large outdoor terrace area at the center of campus. This was a highschool, so my favorite class was AP Calculus AB.

Stevens Institute of Technology

A school located at the heart of an urban mecca and centered around the Brutalist Howe Center. As I am still a student here, my favorite class so far has been Human Computer Interaction.

My favorite memory at this school has been witnessing professors back track out of mildy racist comments.
